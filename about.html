ABOUT ME

I'm a seasoned Geospatial Developer with a strong background in Geographic Information Science and Environmental Science. My journey in the geospatial field began in China at FlashData, where I honed my skills in geospatial data processing and analysis, and developed an automated data extraction tool using Python that significantly reduced manual processing time. My passion for creating detailed topographic maps and 3D terrain models contributed to a 30% increase in project approval rates.

I then moved on to CNSA, where I designed and executed spatial data analysis projects for over 100 clients, achieving a client satisfaction rate of 95%. My work in developing interactive 3D maps and visualizations using WebGL improved user interaction and data interpretation by 35%. I also had the opportunity to conduct a comprehensive geospatial data audit for a major metropolitan area, which resulted in a 50% improvement in data reliability.

My most recent role was at M.Tech in Singapore, where I led the development of a real-time geospatial data visualization platform using three.js and WebGL. This resulted in a 45% increase in user engagement and a 30% reduction in data processing time. I also managed a team of 12 developers and GIS specialists in creating a comprehensive 3D city model, which improved urban planning decision-making processes by 25%.

In addition to my professional experience, I hold a Master of Science in Geographic Information Science from Clark University, Worcester, MA, and a Bachelor of Arts in Environmental Science. I have also published 10 research papers on advanced geospatial techniques and 3D visualization methodologies in peer-reviewed journals.

I am a results-driven professional with a proven track record of delivering innovative solutions and leading technical teams. My expertise lies in strategic planning, project management, and aligning IT initiatives with business objectives to drive growth and efficiency . I am always eager to learn and adapt to new technologies and challenges in the geospatial field.
